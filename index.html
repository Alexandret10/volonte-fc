01 — The Project

We are building a football academy from the ground up. On one side, we develop the talent. On the other, we deliver the programmes that fund and shape it. Two halves of the same pitch, moving toward one goal: teams that travel, players who rise, and a club with a name that carries.

03 — The Vision

Built in waves.

Volonté is designed to grow in deliberate stages, inspired by multi club models like City Football Group and Red Bull. Each wave lays the groundwork for the next.

Wave 01

Foundation

Incorporation, brand, and the first programmes. Private coaching, after school skills, and small sided games launch in Markham and Toronto.

In progress
Wave 02

Teams that travel

Competitive squads formed across age groups, taken on the road to play tournaments and represent the crest beyond local play.

Next
Wave 03

Provincial expansion

New sites and partnerships extend Volonté across Ontario and into other provinces, building a recognised Canada wide identity.

Horizon
Wave 04

The multi club model

International hubs and a connected family of clubs sharing one philosophy, one method, and one ambition.
